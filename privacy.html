Parent Tasker App's Privacy Policy

Last updated Nov 16, 2020

Parent Tasker App respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the “Last updated” date of this Privacy Policy. You are encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates. You will be deemed to have been made aware of, will be subject to, and will be deemed to have accepted the changes in any revised Privacy Policy by your continued use of the Application after the date such revised Privacy Policy is posted.

Collection of Your Information

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Application depends on the content and materials you use, and includes:

Personal Data

Registration for using the app is compulsory. The app stores only users' name/username/nickname on the device you are accessing this app from. Your data will be accessible only to you or whoever use the device on which the app is installed.

Financial Data

We do not collect your credit card or bank details or any other financial information.

Data from Social Networks

This app does not collect any information from any of your social networking websites.

Geo-Location Information

We do not request access or permission to and track location-based information from your mobile device, either continuously or while you are using the Application, to provide location-based services.

Mobile Device Access

We may request permission to access photos from your mobile device. If you wish to change our access or permissions, you may do so in your device’s settings. While using the app, we may also access your device's screen size and operating system information to optimize the app's performance but again we do not store such information in our server.

Push Notifications

We do not send you push notifications regarding your account or the Application.

Third-Party Data

We do not collect any personal data and do not provide such data to third parties.

We do not collect any personal data. But, we may use anonymous statistical data about the app usage to:

  1. Assist law enforcement and respond to subpoena.

  3. Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Application.
  4. Notify you of updates to the Application.
  5. Prevent fraudulent transactions, monitor against theft, and protect against criminal activity.
  6. Request feedback and contact you about your use of the Application.
  7. Resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems.
